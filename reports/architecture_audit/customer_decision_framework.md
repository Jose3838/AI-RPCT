# Customer Decision Framework

## Core Mission

AI-RPCT helps organizations make better AI infrastructure decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------

## Executive Questions

Every screen in AI-RPCT should answer at least one of these questions.

### Capacity

Do we have enough AI capacity?

Should we reserve more?

------------------------------------------------------------

### Procurement

Should we buy now?

Should we wait?

Which GPU?

Which provider?

------------------------------------------------------------

### Risk

Where is our biggest infrastructure risk?

Which supplier is becoming unreliable?

------------------------------------------------------------

### Competition

What is changing in the market?

Who is growing?

Who is losing?

------------------------------------------------------------

### Investment

Where should we invest?

Which technology has the strongest outlook?

------------------------------------------------------------

### Forecast

What is likely to happen during the next

30 days

90 days

180 days

365 days

------------------------------------------------------------

### Strategy

If I do nothing today…

What is the likely outcome?

------------------------------------------------------------

Golden Rule

Every dashboard should improve at least one decision.
