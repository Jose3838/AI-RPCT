# AI-RPCT Product Vision 2030

## Vision

Become the world's leading Decision Intelligence Platform for AI Infrastructure.

------------------------------------------------------------

## Mission

Enable organizations to make faster, safer and smarter AI infrastructure decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------

## Long-Term Objectives

AI-RPCT should become the trusted source for:

- GPU market intelligence
- Provider intelligence
- Capacity intelligence
- AI infrastructure forecasting
- Procurement recommendations
- Infrastructure risk analysis
- Executive decision support

------------------------------------------------------------

## Product Principles

1. Every decision must be explainable.

2. Every forecast must include confidence.

3. Every recommendation must include evidence.

4. Every dataset must have lineage.

5. Every signal must be reproducible.

6. Trust is more important than speed.

------------------------------------------------------------

## Long-Term Positioning

AI-RPCT is not a monitoring platform.

AI-RPCT is not a dashboard.

AI-RPCT is a Decision Intelligence Platform.
