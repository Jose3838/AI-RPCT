# AI-RPCT Terms Draft

AI-RPCT is currently offered as a technical preview.

The product provides research signals related to AI infrastructure, GPU availability, provider concentration and compute market stress.

AI-RPCT does not provide financial advice, investment advice, legal advice or procurement advice.

Current limitations:

- Some provider data may be placeholder or experimental.
- Forecasts are experimental.
- Signals should be independently verified before business decisions.
