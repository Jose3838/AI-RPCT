# AI-RPCT

The AI Infrastructure Risk Intelligence Platform.

AI-RPCT helps teams monitor GPU scarcity, compute pricing pressure, provider concentration, and AI infrastructure risk.

## For AI Startups

Forecast compute cost pressure before it hits your budget.

## For Funds

Track AI infrastructure stress before the market prices it in.

## For GPU Providers

Benchmark supply pressure and competitive positioning.

## Core Metrics

- RPCT Score
- GPU Shortage Probability
- Provider Leaderboard
- Market Share
- Concentration Index
- Alerts
