# Design Partner Feedback Loop

AI-RPCT should validate the Bloomberg-style product with design partners before scaling paid infrastructure.

## Weekly Loop

1. Send the customer-ready executive brief.
2. Ask which signal changed a real decision.
3. Ask which signal was confusing or not trusted.
4. Ask what alert would make them open AI-RPCT tomorrow morning.
5. Record every answer in `docs/CUSTOMER_DISCOVERY.md`.

## Success Criteria

- At least 3 target users review the research preview.
- At least 1 user asks for a recurring weekly brief.
- At least 1 user names a specific buying, risk, or monitoring decision affected by AI-RPCT.
- No paid claim is made before claim gates are paid-safe.

## CTO Rule

If feedback does not improve the daily decision product, it is not roadmap signal yet.
