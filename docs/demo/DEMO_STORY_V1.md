# AI-RPCT Demo Story

## Problem

GPU pricing is fragmented across providers.

Nobody has a historical intelligence layer that tracks:

- market share
- provider dominance
- supply shifts
- price forecasts
- market regimes

## Solution

AI-RPCT continuously collects and analyzes the GPU cloud market.

## What AI-RPCT knows

- 3682+ historical observations
- 23 GPU models
- 6 providers
- forecast history
- market depth history
- provider dominance history
- regime history

## Key Screens

1. Intelligence Summary
2. Strategy Dashboard
3. CEO Command Center
4. Forecast Accuracy Trend
5. Data Moat Dashboard

## Outcome

AI-RPCT transforms raw GPU listings into market intelligence.
